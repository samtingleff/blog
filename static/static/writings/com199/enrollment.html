Communication Enrollment Procedures

How to Change or Declare Your Major

The form to change or declare the major is obtained at the Registrar's office.
The form is titled, "Undergraduate Change of Major Request", it's a single page
document, easy to complete and does not require the department's approval.

Only declared majors may telephone register into Communication courses. If
you haven't declared and telephone enrollment (TeSS) is about to begin or in
progress, go to the Registrar's and complete the form. As you submit the form
let them know you wish to have the 'hold' lifted so you may TeSS immediately.
The form will be processed promptly allowing you to TeSS instantly, otherwise,
you will not be cleared to TeSS until the following pre-enrollment period.

Enrollment Procedure

  • Telephone enrollment (TeSS)
    1. Call the TeSS phone number: 534-4000 (TDD 822-1699)
      (If using an on-campus Ericsson digital phone, press the Dial
      Additional Digits button before entering your Personal ID number.)
    2. Enter your Personal ID Number.
    3. Enter your Personal Access Code.
    4. When prompted, enter your first Section ID and wait for
      a response. If the course has multiple grading options or
      variable units, the system will prompt you to enter your
      choice at the appropriate time.

    Most Communication courses are set up on the computer
    to recognize only Communication declared majors who have
    completed the prerequisites. If not qualified, permission
    from the instructor and a department stamp will allow you
    to enroll.

  • Enroll in your classes via WebReg

    By using your Student ID number and PAC (the same ones
    you use for telephone enrollment) you can add, drop, change,
    and wait list sections on-line.

To Add a Course

  1. Attend the course you wish to add and get the professor's permission and
    have him/her sign an add/drop card. If the class you wish to add is a course with
    sections/discussions you need the TA's signature instead of the professor's.
  2. Bring the card to the main office for a department stamp.
  3. Submit it to the Registrar's.
    The Registrar's will not process your add card without the department stamp.
    Also, if you happened to get the professor's signature before classes begin, save
    the card. The department will not issue a stamp until classes begin. If you take
    the card to the Registrar's before classes begin without a department stamp, the
    card will be taken from you and thrown away. Save your card and do not worry,
    the signature secures you a place in class until you can properly enroll.

How to Obtain Grades

The department does not list nor give grades by telephone or otherwise. Grades
are available on TeSS approximately two weeks after the end of final week for
the term. Check the Calendar in the front of the Schedule of Classes for the
projected date. Contact the department regarding unreported grades. Unreported
grades will lapse to failing if not cleared within one quarter.

How to Contest a Grade

Speak to the professor or Teacher Assistant who issued the grade. If it isn't
resolved to your satisfaction refer to the UCSD general catalog for detailed
information on how to proceed.
