This questionaire was given to undergraduate students in Professor Carol Padden's Senior Seminar class. Similar questions were submitted to Joshua Polterock's Communicating and Computers class email list. The results from these surveys form the basis for my conclusions.

  1. Have you seen the department web page?
  2. How often do you use it? (times/week,times/month)
  3. What do you look for?
  4. Would you like to see new faculty introduced on the web page?
  5. What should be there that is not?
  6. Would you like to receive email announcements about the Communication department?
  7. Are there other campus web pages that you find useful? Why?
