Last weekend before school

We saw a ton of movies this weekend.
Garden State with the guy from Scrubs and Natalie Portman. Let's face it, with those two you can hardly go wrong. Not much else going for it though.
The Bourne Supremacy. Exactly what you would expect but well done. I liked it, of course.
Before Sunset, the sequel to Before Sunrise from 1995. If you liked the first, go see this. If not, rent Sunrise then see Sunset. They're both very charming and always made me think of Breean in Paris.

My wife Breean disappeared yesterday. Someone else unlocked our front door and came into the house claiming to be her. This is what she looks like. It may not be Breean but I think she's pretty cute anyway.
