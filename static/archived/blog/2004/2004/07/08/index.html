Mystery of the Consul General's wife

I'm having dinner tonight at the Norwegian consulate's house in San Francisco. How did that happen, you ask...

The class I'm taking this summer is 90% Norwegian students, most of whom found the program through the Norwegian School of Entrepreneurship. This school shares an office with the consulate and seems to have close ties with him.

The consulate generously offered to host the entire class for dinner tonight. The invitation says:

The Consul General of Norway
Are-Jostein Norheim and Mrs. Susana Lactoo Norheim
request the pleasure of the company of
...

Just like that, with his wife's name crossed out. Why? What happened?

Update: turns out his wife is just out of town. Crossing her name off the invitation still seems a little strange, no? Would Miss Manners approve?

The Consul General has a very nice house, btw.
