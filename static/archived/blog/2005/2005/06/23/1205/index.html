Supernanny

Things we have learned from watching Supernanny...

  1. Do not punish children by sending them to the bedroom. If they have a fun room with lots of toys this is hardly a punishment. In any case, you're sending a confusing message: is the bedroom a place to sleep or a site of punishment? Use a "naughty corner" or a "naughty room" like a guest bedroom. When you let them out, squat to their level and make them apologize.
  2. Sit down to their level and give eye contact when disciplining. Make sure they understand why they're being disciplined.
  3. Do not allow naps longer than 1.5 hours - they'll never fall asleep at night.
  4. When they sulk - probably when finally leaving the naughty corner - ignore it and be very positive.
  5. Keep a very strict schedule. Kids should eat, play, nap, etc. at the same times every day.
